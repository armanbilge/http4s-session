    Http4s Session Management

    Quick Start

    To use http4s-session in an existing SBT project with Scala 2.13 or a later version, add the following dependencies to your build.sbt depending on your needs:

    libraryDependencies ++= Seq(
  "org.http4s" %% "http4s-session" % "<version>"
)
